Bio

I am a consultant who found a deeper passion for software engineering while working with ERP systems in manufacturing and supply chain environments. Those roles gave me a clear view of how business processes connect with technology, and they encouraged me to look beyond support work and move toward building solutions myself. Over time, that curiosity grew into a steady drive to learn, experiment, and understand software at a more fundamental level.

Studying the Master of Software Engineering in Auckland has given me a fresh perspective and a stronger foundation. Working closely with development teams helps me refine my skills and strengthens my confidence as an engineer. For me, learning is not just about completing courses but about seeing how ideas turn into working systems and how thoughtful design can make everyday tasks easier.
I enjoy challenges that push me a little further and reveal new angles to explore. People often notice that me when I am focused, I bring calm energy and patience, even when problems get complex. My long-term goal is to become a capable software engineer who contributes to meaningful projects and eventually builds a company that supports innovation and creativity. Growth, curiosity, and consistent effort shape me. I believe progress comes from small steps taken with intention, and I look forward to the paths that open as I continue to learn and build.
